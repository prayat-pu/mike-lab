Dr. Petch Sajjacholapunt is a researcher at the Machine Intelligence and Knowledge Engineering (MIKE) Research Clusters at Mahidol University.

### Biography
Dr. Petch Sajjacholapunt is a Lecturer and currently serves as the Deputy Dean for Administration and Human Resources at the Faculty of ICT, Mahidol University. His research interests include Technology-Enhanced Learning, Recommendation Systems, and Image Processing.

### Education
* **Ph.D. in Computer Science**, University of Warwick, United Kingdom
* **M.Phil. in Computer Science**, The University of Manchester, United Kingdom
* **M.Sc. in Computer Science**, The University of Manchester, United Kingdom
* **B.Sc. in Information and Communication Technology**, Mahidol University, Thailand
