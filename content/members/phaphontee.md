### Biography
I am a PhD student in Computer Science with research interests in mathematical logic, computational logic in AI, algorithmic machine learning, and data science. My current work focuses on learning representations of incomplete tabular data using graph neural networks. I am interested in research that combines mathematical rigor with practical machine learning applications, especially in problems involving structured data and feature interactions. I am open to collaboration with researchers and professionals in AI, machine learning, and related fields.
