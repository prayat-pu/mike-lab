Assistant Professor Dr. Tipajin Thaipisutikul is a researcher at the Machine Intelligence and Knowledge Engineering (MIKE) Research Clusters and MVIT Research LAB at Mahidol University.

### Biography
Assistant Professor Dr. Tipajin Thaipisutikul's research primarily focuses on advanced artificial intelligence and data science, including deep learning, sequence learning, and applied intelligence.

### Education
* **Ph.D. in Computer Science (1st Class Honor)**, National Central University (NCU), Taiwan
* **M.Sc. in Information Technology (2nd Class Honor)**, University of Sydney (USYD), Australia
* **B.Sc. in Information and Communication Technology (1st Class Honor)**, Mahidol University, Thailand
