Beyond the world of deep learning and knowledge engineering, the **MIKE Research Clusters** family knows the importance of good food and great company. 

Recently, our team took a break from the lab to enjoy a delicious **Korean BBQ dinner at Baan Dee**. It was a wonderful opportunity for our researchers—from our Principal Investigator to our PhD and Master's students—to connect outside the academic environment, share stories, and strengthen our team bond over sizzling grills.

These moments of relaxation and camaraderie are essential to our lab culture. They fuel our creativity and collaborative spirit, helping us tackle the complex research challenges we work on every day. 

A big thank you to everyone who joined! We are already looking forward to our next team outing.
