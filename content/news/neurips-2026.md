We are thrilled to announce that the **MIKE Research Clusters** will be well-represented at **NeurIPS 2026** in Vancouver. Three of our primary research tracks—Precision Health, Social Intelligence, and Systemic Discovery—each secured a spot in the main track.

These papers highlight our commitment to engineering AI solutions that are not only theoretically sound but also ready for real-world deployment. Congratulations to the student leads and faculty mentors involved in these high-impact projects.
